1. UVSS (Under Vehicle Surveillance System)

This cutting-edge surveillance system is designed for military and high-security zones. It features AI-powered image analysis, solar/tree-powered cameras, and real-time Wi-Fi data transmission. Already deployed at the Indian Army base in Navshera, J&K, it captures under-vehicle images and number plates of incoming trucks and compares them on return to detect foreign objects or threats such as mines or hidden weapons. The system can also be customized for use at malls and government buildings.

2. AI-Powered Human Behavior Detection

This system leverages AI to analyze real-time human activity and detect suspicious behaviors. It recognizes movements, facial expressions, and hidden weapons using intelligent algorithms. Perfect for use in airports, lounges, malls, and critical infrastructure zones. It prevents unauthorized access and potential threats with automated alerts and behavior profiling.

3. Stealth Combat Camera System

Developed for ambush and battlefield scenarios, this miniature camera system is powered by the potential difference of water flow in tree trunks and enhanced by solar backup. These camouflaged button-sized cameras activate on motion, create a Wi-Fi mesh to relay data, and feed real-time footage to AI-powered servers that identify armed personnel or intruders. Designed for Indian Army use, it ensures stealth intelligence without human risk.

4. Smart Data Extraction Systems

AI models extract structured data from documents (PDF, DOC, XML, CSV, Excel) and images using OCR and AI. Runs entirely on standalone Linux servers, removing cloud dependency and ensuring data privacy. Ideal for banks, legal firms, logistics, and healthcare sectors. Fully customizable for domain-specific data formats and workflows.

5. Customized AI/ML Models

Our AI/ML models are deployed on-premises using Linux-based Docker images. They offer automated data extraction, report generation, receipt creation, and live chatbot services. The system is hackproof with no open ports, encrypted using quantum cryptography, and integrated with biometric multi-factor authentication. Designed for secure sectors like defense and banking, where access requires biometrics from multiple high-authority personnel for verification.
